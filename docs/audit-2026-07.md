Per your stated read: **< 30s = machine** (616 rows, 27.9%) — the camera re-reading a card still in frame, or a volunteer re-scanning because the first scan gave no visible feedback. **> 5min = human** (901 rows, 40.9%) — a real second visit; on a collection list that's a correct refusal. The **30s–5min band (687 rows, 31.2%) is ambiguous** — could be either a slow fumble-triggered stale re-read or a genuine quick return visit; not assigned either way here.
