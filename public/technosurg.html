Message from the Chairman

Greetings from GEM Hospitals.

It is our privilege to invite you to GEM TECHNOSURG 2026. As surgical practice continues to evolve with the integration of Artificial Intelligence, robotic platforms, and Fluorescence guided surgery, there is an increasing need for structured learning and hands-on exposure.

"Surgery is no longer defined by technique alone,
but by technology and insight."

TECHNOSURG 2026 is designed as a focused academic platform featuring renowned national and international faculty, delivering expert-led sessions, panel discussions, and immersive training experiences.

The conference will highlight the practical application of emerging technologies, enabling participants to integrate these advancements into everyday surgical practice. A key component of the program includes robotic simulation and skill-based training modules, providing an opportunity to gain direct experience in a guided environment.

We invite you to be part of this academic initiative and engage with the future direction of surgery.

"Learning today. Leading tomorrow."
Prof. C. Palanivelu
Chairman, GEM Hospitals
